Executive Summary

My Organization's MDM.MDM_INTERFACES schema is a mature, production-grade Master Data Management hub serving as the authoritative source for customer, vendor, employee, and party data across Snowflake's internal operational systems (Salesforce, Workday, ServiceNow, GitHub). The deployment is exceptionally strong for Third-Party Risk Management, 360 Account Intelligence, and MDM/Entity Resolution — three use cases where it is genuinely best-in-class with 4 deployment-specific READY capabilities. Against the 10 canonical Technology/SaaS use cases (which skew toward product telemetry and billing), the canonical fit score is 2.96/10, reflecting that this schema is a data operations backbone powering other analytics layers, not a product event store. The three highest-priority opportunities are: (1) activate a vendor risk posture dashboard from already-present SecurityScorecard + Panorays + SOE data, (2) build a Revenue Intelligence EACV waterfall from the rich DIM_USE_CASE + FACT_USE_CASE_STAGE_MOVEMENT already deployed, and (3) connect MDM account master to consumption data to enable Customer Health Scoring.
